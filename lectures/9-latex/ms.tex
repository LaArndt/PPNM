\section{Introduction}

The gamma function do be a function, that is math stuff:
\begin{equation}\label{eq-def}
	\Gamma(z) = \int_0^\infty x^{z-1} e^{-x}\,dx, \ \qquad \Re(z) > 0\ .
\end{equation}
